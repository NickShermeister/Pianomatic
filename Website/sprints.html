Sprint 1

Our first iteration of Pianomatic was a box with infrared sensors on the back and servos in the front. A servo plate had the servos aligned in a V-formation with arms attached to the servos. The infrared sensors were capable of reading digital inputs to move the appropriate servo. The code implemented allowed for as many servos as an Arduino could support for piano playing. A rough draft design of our website was created allowing us to document our progress through the coming weeks. Having completed a rough design of the Pianomatic we planned on having enough servos and infrared sensors for playing an octave. Future progress would be on creating a scroller and creating encoded notes for the sensors to detect and play. We believe the scroller would create the most trouble as we had not created a design for the scroller or the music notes.
